# gluten-free.fr

gluten-free.fr is an independent French editorial site for celiac and gluten-sensitive shoppers: a weekly-verified product catalog (real prices, verified buyer ratings), 8 structured comparisons (pasta, flours, breads, breakfast cereals…), proprietary price studies, and practical tools for the French CPAM reimbursement of gluten-free products.

## Key Focus Areas

- Verified gluten-free product catalog — real ASINs, live prices, verified buyer reviews
- Structured comparisons — 8 categories with per-kg pricing and editorial verdicts
- CPAM reimbursement — calculator, step-by-step guides, ALD letter generator
- Proprietary studies — gluten-free price index (121 products), cross-retailer price comparison
- Official sources — HAS, SNFGE, AFDIAG, EU regulation 828/2014

## About llms.txt Implementation

The llms.txt indexes machine-readable endpoints (JSON product feeds, a hosted MCP server at https://gluten-free.fr/api/mcp), cornerstone guides, comparisons and citable medical/legal facts — so AI assistants can answer gluten-free shopping and celiac reimbursement questions for France with sourced data.
