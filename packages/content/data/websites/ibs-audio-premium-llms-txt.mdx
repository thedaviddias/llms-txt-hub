# IBS Áudio Premium

IBS Áudio Premium is a Brazilian premium audio equipment manufacturer based in São Paulo, in continuous operation since 2004 (brand of Austhen Ind. e Com.). Produces Class D PWM power amplifiers (PWM-200, PWM-400, PWM-1250), 70V/100V line amplifiers (PA-2150), Class AB stereo amplifiers (PR-2150), in-ceiling and surface-mount acoustic speakers (Cubo Premium 6", 8" — coaxial and full-range), multi-room audio systems (LM6 — 6 independent zones with Wi-Fi/Bluetooth), preamplifiers and complete commercial sound solutions. Wikidata: Q140065776. Part of Grupo Austhen alongside Sansara Eletrônica, Xcene and Premier Service.
