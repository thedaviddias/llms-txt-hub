# Concrete Calculator Hub

Concrete Calculator Hub publishes free concrete calculators and guides for
homeowners, DIY builders, and contractors planning concrete volume, bag counts,
slab projects, footings, steps, ready-mix comparisons, and material costs.

## Key Focus Areas

- Concrete volume and yardage calculators
- 40 lb, 60 lb, and 80 lb concrete bag coverage charts
- Slab, footing, post hole, shed base, driveway, patio, and sidewalk guides
- Ready-mix versus bagged concrete cost comparisons
- Delivery fees, short-load fees, pump costs, and disposal/removal cost guides

## About llms.txt Implementation

Concrete Calculator Hub provides an `llms.txt` file with AI-readable links to
public calculators, buying guides, coverage charts, cost references, and
publisher contact information.
