# Systemische Beratung Rheinbach

A German counseling practice led by Nicole van de Meer, offering systemic therapy and family counseling services in Rheinbach and surrounding areas.

## Services Offered

- Individual counseling for personal challenges
- Couples therapy for relationship issues
- Family counseling for conflict resolution
- Parenting advice and support
- Crisis intervention and life transitions

## Approach

The practice follows a systemic approach focusing on resources rather than deficits, understanding before solutions, and practical applicability in everyday life.

## Service Area

Serving clients in Rheinbach, Meckenheim, Euskirchen and surrounding areas (up to 20km radius). Sessions available online or in-person.
