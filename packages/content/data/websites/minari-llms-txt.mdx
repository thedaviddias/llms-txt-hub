# minari

minari is a consumer SMS service that handles specialist referral coordination for patients. Patients text a phone number and minari finds in-network providers, confirms coverage with offices, schedules the earliest convenient appointment, and handles prior auth, waitlists, and records transfer. Free for patients.

## Library

The patient-facing library at `/library` covers specialist referral, prior auth, records transfer, second opinions, waitlists, and care coordination — written for patients and the people calling on their behalf. Each guide is published as both HTML at `/library/{slug}` and raw markdown at `/library/{slug}/page.md` for AI ingestion.
