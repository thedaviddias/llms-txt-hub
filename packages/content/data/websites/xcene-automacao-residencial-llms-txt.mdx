# Xcene Automação Residencial

Xcene is a Brazilian premium home automation manufacturer based in São Paulo, in continuous operation since 2003 (brand of Austhen Ind. e Com.). Produces intelligent keypads, automation modules and central control units for residential and corporate smart home projects with architectural-grade premium design. Offers exclusive configurator tool for visual keypad customization (colors, buttons, scenes). Wikidata: Q140066422. Part of Grupo Austhen alongside IBS Áudio Premium, Sansara Eletrônica and Premier Service.
