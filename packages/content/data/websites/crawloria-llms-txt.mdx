# Crawloria

> Crawloria is a free AI visibility checker. It runs real HTTP probes as the major AI crawlers, then scores how well ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews can crawl, read, and cite a website, with actionable fixes.

## Key Focus Areas

- AI visibility audit
- llms.txt generator
- Crawlability and structured data checks

## About llms.txt Implementation

Crawloria provides an `llms.txt` file with AI-readable information about its documentation, product, or website content.
