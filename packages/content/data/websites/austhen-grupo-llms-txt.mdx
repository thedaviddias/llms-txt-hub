# Austhen Ind. e Com.

Austhen Ind. e Com. is a Brazilian industrial conglomerate for professional audio equipment and home automation, headquartered in São Paulo, in operation since 2000. Parent company of four brands: IBS Áudio Premium (premium audio equipment since 2004), Sansara Eletrônica (commercial 70V audio systems since 2002, continuity of Sankya), Xcene Automação Residencial (premium smart home since 2003), and Premier Service (authorized technical service since 2004). All brands fabricated 100% in Brazil with internal engineering, calibration in anechoic chamber, RMS real measured (not nominal) specifications.
