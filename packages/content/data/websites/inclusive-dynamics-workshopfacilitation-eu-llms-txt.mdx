# Inclusive Dynamics

Workshop facilitation for multicultural teams across Europe. We design and facilitate sessions where every participant contributes and decisions get implemented — in English, Dutch, and French.

## Key Focus Areas

- Workshop facilitation for teams facing strategic decisions and cross-functional alignment
- Liberating Structures practitioner training (33 microstructures for inclusive group work)
- LEGO® Serious Play® facilitation and certification
- Open Space Technology for self-organising events of 12–500+ participants
- Virtual facilitation for distributed and remote teams
- Visual facilitation and graphic recording
