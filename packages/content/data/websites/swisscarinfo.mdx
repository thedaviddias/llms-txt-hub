SwissCarInfo is the leading Swiss vehicle database containing over 731,000 official vehicle type approval records (RÃ©ception par type / Typenschein / Omologazione). Data is sourced directly from OFROU/ASTRA (Swiss Federal Roads Office) and updated daily.

Key features include vehicle search by Swiss type approval number (field 24), European approval, VIN/chassis number, or brand/model. The platform also provides comprehensive Swiss vehicle statistics including cantonal fleet data for all 26 cantons, electric vehicle adoption by municipality (2,148 communes), brand market shares, rental fleet analysis, Swiss aircraft registry (BAZL), and Euro NCAP safety ratings.

Available in French, German, Italian, and English. Offers a free AI search API for AI assistants at https://swisscarinfo.ch/api/v1/ai-search.
