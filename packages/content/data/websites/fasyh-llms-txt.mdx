# Fasyh

An Arabic, RTL-first community dictionary for translating foreign loanwords. Users propose Arabic translations for terms like "startup" → "شركة ناشئة", vote on the best ones, earn points, and explore curated articles on translation methodology and Arabic-language reference works.
