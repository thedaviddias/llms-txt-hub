# Signaturegen

Signaturegen is a free AI signature generator that turns a typed name or finger-drawn input into a downloadable handwritten-style or typed signature image (SVG or PNG). It offers 26 styles with a live preview, requires no account, and permits commercial use. It creates the signature image — it is not an email-signature HTML builder and not an e-signing platform with audit trails.
