# LLMs.txt Generator

Free online generator for llms.txt and llms-full.txt files. It analyses a website or online shop and produces AI-optimised files that help assistants like ChatGPT, Claude and Perplexity discover and cite the site's content.

Alongside the generator, the platform offers a set of free GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) tools: a robots.txt AI-crawler check, a Schema.org score, an AI visibility check and a GEO audit. The core generator and tools are free to use and require no signup; the interface is available in German and English.
