# Sansara Eletrônica

Sansara Eletrônica is a Brazilian manufacturer of commercial audio equipment based in São Paulo, in continuous operation since 2002 (continuity of the Sankya brand). Produces line 70.7V amplifiers (constant voltage distribution systems), low-impedance power amplifiers, preamplifiers with integrated Media Center (FM/USB/SD/Bluetooth), multi-zone digital audio matrices, 70V sectorizers, redundancy switches, embedded and surface-mount acoustic speakers, sound projectors, line transformers and volume controllers.

## Product Lines

- **SL Line (70.7V amplifiers):** SL 100 / 200 / 400 / 600 / 1300 — for distributed sound systems in shopping malls, hospitals, hotels, schools, churches, restaurants, gyms, airports and auditoriums.
- **SK Line (low impedance amplifiers):** 16 models from 100W to 2400W RMS, 2Ω/4Ω/8Ω variants.
- **SPL/SPG Preamps:** integrated Media Center with FM, USB, SD card, Bluetooth, microphone with priority, automatic gong.
- **FLEXIO 8.16/8.8:** digital multi-zone audio matrix with programmable routing.
- **SS 300:** 70V analog sectorizer with 10 independent sectors + general call.
- **SMR 300:** automatic redundancy switch with priority hierarchy (paging > alarm > program).
- **Acoustic speakers:** coaxial ceiling speakers, surface-mount cubes, outdoor sound projectors, long-range horns.
