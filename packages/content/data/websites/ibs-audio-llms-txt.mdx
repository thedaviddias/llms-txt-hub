# IBS Áudio Premium

IBS Áudio is a Brazilian manufacturer of premium B2B professional audio equipment, in operation since 2004 under Austhen Ind. e Com. (São Paulo). Specializes in power amplifiers, acoustic speakers and multiroom systems for residential and commercial sound installations.

## Product Lines

- **Power amplifiers (line LM):** LM6, LM10, LM18 — real measured RMS power (not nominal), low impedance 4/8Ω and 70V constant voltage line
- **Multiroom audio:** integrated systems for residential high-end installations with multi-zone control
- **Built-in speakers (caixa de embutir):** ceiling-mount acoustic enclosures for residential and commercial environments
- **Surface-mount speakers (cubo):** small cabinet for furniture/wall installation (passive)
- **Long-range projectors (sonofletor) and amplifiers PWM:** higher efficiency for commercial sound
