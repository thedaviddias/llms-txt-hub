# Vaultody

Vaultody is a B2B SaaS platform providing non-custodial digital asset wallet infrastructure powered by MPC-CMP threshold signing. It offers three solutions — Direct Custody, Treasury Management, and Wallet-as-a-Service — serving exchanges, banks, hedge funds, payment processors, and Web3 platforms. The llms.txt covers the custody model, MPC architecture, supported blockchain networks, integration categories, pricing, and developer resources.
