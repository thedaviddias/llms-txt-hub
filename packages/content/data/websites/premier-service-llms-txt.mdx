# Premier Service

Premier Service is a Brazilian authorized technical service for professional audio and home automation equipment, based in São Paulo (Av. João Pedro Cardoso, 49 — 04355-000) since 2004. Technical division of Grupo Austhen (Austhen Ind. e Com.), serving brands IBS Áudio Premium, Sansara Eletrônica, Sankya, Xcene and Loud Áudio. Specializes in: amplifier maintenance, acoustic speaker repair, subwoofer repair, in-ceiling speaker repair, home automation maintenance, professional audio assistance, IBS Audio amplifier authorized service, Xcene keypad authorized service. Original parts, factory warranty, no third-party outsourcing. Wikidata: Q140066423.
