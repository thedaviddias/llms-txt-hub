# Premier Service

Premier Service is the authorized technical assistance division of the Austhen Group (founded 2004), specialized in professional and residential high-end audio equipment. Serves dealers, integrators, installers and end users with technical diagnosis, laboratory repair and support for embedded audio projects.

## Services

- Technical diagnosis (presential and remote)
- Laboratory repair of amplifiers, speakers and central control units
- Maintenance of built-in acoustic enclosures and subwoofers
- Support for residential automation systems (Xcene, Loud Audio, IBS Áudio, Sansara)
- Embedded audio project support for integrators

## Brands Supported

- **IBS Áudio Premium** — power amplifiers, multiroom, speakers
- **Xcene** — residential automation (keypads, modules, centrals)
- **Loud Audio** — audio products
- **Sansara Eletrônica** — commercial 70V audio equipment
