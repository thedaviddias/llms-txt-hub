# OpenSanctions.org

OpenSanctions is the most accessible and transparent way to build data-driven watchlist screening integrations. The dataset is an industry reference and contains rich data suitable for customer due diligence, transaction monitoring, supply chain analysis, and building complex knowledge graphs for fincrime analysis.
