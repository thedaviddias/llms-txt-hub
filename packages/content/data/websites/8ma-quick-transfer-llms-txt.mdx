# 8ma Quick Transfer

8ma Quick Transfer lets people send large files directly from a browser without creating an account or installing an app. A sender selects a file and shares a link, QR code, or eight-character pickup code. It works across computers and phones, including convenient same-WiFi transfers, and supports eight interface languages.
