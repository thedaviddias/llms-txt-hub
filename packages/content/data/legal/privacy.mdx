# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: August 27, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how llms.txt hub, operated by David Dias Digital ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, processes, and protects your personal information when you use our website (llmstxthub.com) and related services (collectively, the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Information**: When you create an account via SSO, we collect your email address and profile information from your authentication provider
- **Website Submissions**: URLs, descriptions, and metadata when you submit websites to our directory
- **Communications**: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to us
- **Newsletter Subscriptions**: Email addresses for our newsletter (managed through Substack)

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Information**: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns, and interaction data
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and IP address
- **Analytics Data**: Website performance and user behavior metrics via Plausible Analytics
- **Authentication Data**: Login sessions and authentication tokens managed by Clerk

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Authentication Providers**: Profile information from GitHub, Google, or other OAuth providers when you sign in
- **Public Data**: Publicly available information about submitted websites and their llms.txt files

## 11. Canadian Privacy Rights (PIPEDA)

As a Canadian-based service, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA):

### 11.1 Your PIPEDA Rights
- **Right to Access**: Request access to your personal information we hold
- **Right to Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for non-essential processing at any time
- **Right to Complain**: File a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

### 11.2 PIPEDA Principles We Follow
- **Accountability**: We are responsible for personal information under our control
- **Identifying Purposes**: We identify why we collect information before or during collection
- **Consent**: We obtain meaningful consent for collection, use, and disclosure
- **Limiting Collection**: We collect only what is necessary for identified purposes
- **Limiting Use and Disclosure**: We use information only for stated purposes
- **Accuracy**: We keep personal information accurate, complete, and up-to-date
- **Safeguards**: We protect information with appropriate security measures
- **Openness**: We make information about our policies readily available
- **Individual Access**: We provide access to your personal information upon request
- **Challenging Compliance**: We provide procedures to challenge our compliance
