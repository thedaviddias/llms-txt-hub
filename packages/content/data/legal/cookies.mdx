# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: August 27, 2025**

This Cookie Policy explains how llms.txt hub, operated by David Dias Digital ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website (llmstxthub.com) and related services.

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

### 1.1 Types of Cookies We Use

- **Session Cookies**: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies**: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
- **First-Party Cookies**: Cookies set by our website directly
- **Third-Party Cookies**: Cookies set by external services we use

## 2. How We Use Cookies

### 2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
| ----------- | ------- | -------- | ---- |
| `__clerk_*` | Authentication and session management | Session/30 days | Essential |

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how visitors use our website:

| Service | Purpose | Data Processing | Opt-Out |
| ------- | ------- | --------------- | ------- |
| Plausible Analytics | Website usage statistics | No personal data, anonymized | Browser settings |

**Note**: Plausible Analytics is privacy-focused and does not use tracking cookies or collect personal data.

### 2.3 Local Storage (Not Cookies)

We use browser local storage (not cookies) to enhance your experience:

| Storage Key | Purpose | Duration | Type |
| ----------- | ------- | -------- | ---- |
| `llms-hub-favorites` | Store your favorite websites locally | Until manually cleared | Local Storage |
| `websites-show-all` | Remember show all preference | Until manually cleared | Local Storage |
| `websites-sort-by` | Remember sort preferences | Until manually cleared | Local Storage |
| `category-sort-by` | Remember category sort preferences | Until manually cleared | Local Storage |
| `recentSearches` | Store recent search history | Until manually cleared | Local Storage |

**Note**: Local storage data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers. This data persists until you clear your browser data or uninstall the browser.

## 3. Cookie Consent

### 3.1 Essential Cookies
Essential cookies are automatically enabled as they are necessary for the website to function. These include:
- Authentication and security cookies
- Basic functionality cookies
- User preference cookies

### 3.2 Non-Essential Cookies
For non-essential cookies, we:
- Provide clear information about their purpose
- Seek your consent before setting them
- Allow you to withdraw consent at any time
- Respect browser "Do Not Track" signals where applicable

## 4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 4.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
- **Edge**: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

### 4.2 Cookie Categories

- **Strictly Necessary**: Cannot be disabled as they are essential for website functionality
- **Analytics**: Can be disabled; will not affect website functionality  
- **Functional**: Can be disabled; may affect some website features

### 4.3 Impact of Disabling Cookies

| Cookie Type | Impact if Disabled |
| ----------- | ------------------ |
| Essential (`__clerk_*`) | Cannot log in or maintain sessions |
| Analytics | No impact on functionality |

### 4.4 Impact of Clearing Local Storage

| Storage Type | Impact if Cleared |
| ------------ | ----------------- |
| Favorites | Will lose all saved favorite websites |
| Sort/Filter Preferences | Will reset to default sort and filter settings |
| Recent Searches | Will lose search history and autocomplete suggestions |
